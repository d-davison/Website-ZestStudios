Low Poly 3D Medieval Weapon Pack

A set of clean, game-ready low poly medieval weapons for Unity. The pack contains 15 models: 9 swords and knives, 3 axes, and 3 hammers.

Every asset has had its origin set to the handle of the weapon, so they're ready to drag and drop straight into your game and parent to a character's hand. Need to tweak something? The FBX source files can be imported into any other 3D software (Maya, Max, Blender, and so on), and materials can be swapped out by creating new materials and dropping them in place of the imported ones.

Features

  • 15 weapons 9 swords & knives, 3 axes, and 3 hammers.
  • Handle-set origins Pivots are placed at the handle, so weapons drag, drop, and parent to a hand cleanly.
  • Low poly & game-ready Lightweight meshes suited to mobile and stylised projects.
  • FBX source files Import and edit in Maya, Max, Blender, or any 3D package.
  • Swappable materials Restyle the weapons by dropping in your own materials.
